What is this?
This tool has two parts. The Animation Builder lets you upload a spritesheet, click frames to build named animation sequences, and export a ready-to-use Animations.json. The Sprite Maker lets you draw your spritesheet from scratch using a pixel editor with layers, then export it as a PNG ready to load into the Animation Builder. Use them together — draw in Sprite Maker, export the sheet, load it into Animation Builder, build your animations.

Spritesheet Format Reference

    
Stardew Valley NPC spritesheets must be exactly 64px wide and a multiple of 32px tall. Each frame is 16×32px. Four frames per row, numbered left→right, top→bottom from 0.

Portrait Reference

    
Portraits are 64×64px per expression, arranged in a 2×3 grid.
